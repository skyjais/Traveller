Operator FAQs

How do I get Paid?

Payments are made every fortnight on the 15th and the last day of the month on completed travel.

How do I receive my bookings?

DIRECT into your booking system. Connect to our Operator API and receive instant confirmation direct into your PMS/Res System or Channel Manager. We are already connected to many of the leading Res Systems and Channel Managers – please see the complete list here

EMAIL – Access our free allocations system called E-bookings. Receive automatic emails of bookings with full reservation and customer details.

PHONE or EMAIL – an agent will either call or send an email to make a reservation directly with you quoting a voucher number to confirm and guarantee payment

How much commission does the agent see?

Operators have ultimate flexibility in allocating commission rates to agents. You can offer one commission rate for all agents or provide different rates for different agents. Our Service Fee is always included in the commission rate you set.

For example: Unique Rate Group 1: if you have set a 27% commission, and the agent is on an AAGT volume-based Service Fee* of 2%, the agent will see and earn 25% commission. If that agent is on an AAGT volume-based Service Fee* of 3%, the agent will see and earn 24% commission.

Unique Rate Group 2: if you have set a 20% commission, and the agent is on an AAGT volume-based Service Fee* of 2%, the agent will see and earn 18% commission. If that agent is on an AAGT volume-based Service Fee* of 3%, the agent will see and earn 17% commission.

*Service Fees are determined on several factors including but not limited to the annual Total Transaction Value of bookings processed by an agent through AAGT and the technology product the bookings are processed through. Volume-based Service Fees are designed to incentivize growth in volume of bookings and support the ongoing development of the technology platforms. A new self-service tool is currently on the Development Roadmap to enable you to create and manage your unique rate groups independently. In the meantime, however, please email travellermailId@gmail.com if you’d like to setup different rates for different agents.

Agent FAQs

How do I take payment when making sales?

You can collect cash, EFT or use our online payment gateway for payment from your customer.
